The Investment Case for Innovation

A factor-based, research-driven approach to capturing innovation while managing risk

Innovation Exposure, Systematically

We pursue innovation exposure through a disciplined, rules-based process—seeking companies that invest in creating the next wave of products, services, and markets—without relying on single-name conviction or forecasts.

  • Signals grounded in observable company data and market microstructure
  • Systematic portfolio construction with liquidity and capacity screens
  • Risk-managed implementation to avoid concentration and style drift

Endogenous Growth Lens

Endogenous growth theory links innovation, human capital, and productivity gains. Academic studies have found that firms leading in innovation have, in many periods, outperformed traditional peers. We draw on this research while preserving our proprietary quantitative edge.

  • Grounded in peer-reviewed literature on innovation and productivity diffusion
  • Focus on reinvestment, knowledge spillovers, and scalable business models
  • Respectful of uncertainty: no promises, just disciplined process and measurement
Signals We Track

R&D intensity, patent quality/velocity, intangible asset growth, and product cycle cadence—balanced against profitability, balance sheet strength, and liquidity.

Risk & Implementation

Position sizing, diversification limits, liquidity screens, and rebalancing discipline to reduce single-name and factor concentration risk while keeping turnover efficient.

Research Rigor

Informed by academic research and practitioner studies, with in-house empirical testing and model validation. We maintain proprietary enhancements we believe add practical edge.

Innovation with Humility and Discipline

Process Over Prediction

We do not forecast outcomes or promise returns. We focus on systematically capturing innovation-related factors with clear rules, transparent data inputs, and repeatable execution.

Proprietary Quantitative Edge

Our proprietary signals and risk controls build on academic foundations while preserving differentiated implementation. The objective is resilient exposure to innovation themes, mindful of drawdowns and real-world constraints.

About Diamond Asset Management

Diamond Asset Management is a registered investment advisor committed to delivering exceptional investment management services and innovative investment solutions.

Our firm combines rigorous quantitative research with disciplined investment management to help clients achieve their financial goals. We specialize in systematic investment strategies and innovation-focused portfolio construction.

Innovation is central to our process. We emphasize measurable, factor-based signals—R&D intensity, intellectual property productivity, and reinvestment in intangibles—rather than narrative-driven bets. We implement these signals with clear rules, liquidity awareness, and diversification.

Our approach is grounded in endogenous growth theory and informed by academic research on innovation and productivity, while preserving proprietary quantitative techniques we believe add practical edge. We do not promise outcomes; we focus on repeatable, evidence-based implementation.
