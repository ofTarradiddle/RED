About DDAM

Diamond & Diamond Asset Management is a registered investment advisor committed to delivering exceptional investment management services and innovative investment solutions.

Our firm combines rigorous quantitative research with disciplined investment management to help clients achieve their financial goals. We specialize in systematic investment strategies and factor-based approaches to portfolio construction.

With deep expertise in factor investing and innovation-focused strategies, we bring institutional-quality research and execution to our advisory relationships. Our team leverages decades of combined experience in quantitative finance, portfolio management, and risk management.
