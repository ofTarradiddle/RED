Customer Relationship Summary

Form CRS - Diamond Asset Management

Introduction

Diamond Asset Management Asset Management (DAM) is registered with the SEC as an investment adviser. Investment advisory and brokerage services and fees differ. Free tools are available at Investor.gov/CRS.

This document provides a brief summary of our services, fees, and legal obligations. For detailed information, please review our Form ADV, Part 2A available on the SEC's IAPD website.

Investment Services

We provide investment advisory services to individuals, families, and institutions. When you invest in our ETFs, you receive standard investment management services at no additional cost beyond the ETF expense ratio.

Additional services (financial planning, tax optimization, custom portfolios) are subject to a separate fee agreement and will be disclosed in your investment advisory agreement.

Standard Services Include: Portfolio management, investment strategy implementation, ongoing monitoring and rebalancing, and regular reporting.

Fees

Standard Services: When you invest in our ETFs, you pay only the ETF expense ratio (33.3 bps / 0.333%). We charge no additional advisory fees for standard investment management services.

Additional services fees are disclosed in your investment advisory agreement. For detailed fee information, review our Form ADV, Part 2A available on the SEC's IAPD website at www.adviserinfo.sec.gov.

Fee Comparison: Our standard services cost significantly less than traditional RIAs that charge separate management fees on top of fund expenses. See our fee comparison charts on the Fees page for detailed analysis.

Legal Obligations

When we act as your investment adviser, we have a fiduciary duty to act in your best interests. We must:

  • Provide investment advice in your best interest
  • Disclose any conflicts of interest
  • Act with utmost good faith
  • Not use your assets for our own benefit

This fiduciary standard is higher than the suitability standard that applies to broker-dealers.

How We Make Money

Our financial professionals are compensated through fees paid by advisory clients. For standard services, compensation comes from the ETF expense ratio. We do not receive commissions or transaction-based compensation.

This fee structure aligns our interests with yours—we succeed when your investments perform well over the long term.

Disciplinary History

No. Neither Diamond Asset Management nor our financial professionals have any legal or disciplinary history that would be material to your evaluation of our advisory services.

Conversation Starters

Ask your financial professional:

  • Given my financial situation, should I choose an investment advisory service? Why or why not?
  • How will you choose investments to recommend to me?
  • What is your relevant experience, including your licenses, education, and other qualifications?
  • What fees will I pay for your services?
  • What are your legal obligations to me when acting as my investment adviser?
  • How else does your firm make money, and what conflicts of interest do you have?
  • How do your financial professionals make money?
  • Do you or your financial professionals have legal or disciplinary history?
