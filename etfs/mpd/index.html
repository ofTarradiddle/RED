Investment Objective

Identify and own high-quality compounding businesses with durable competitive advantages, high returns on invested capital, and management teams with demonstrated capital allocation skill.

The fund uses a quantitative screening process to identify companies exhibiting the vital signs of sustainable compounding: persistently high ROIC, expanding reinvestment opportunities, low capital intensity, and insider alignment. Universe is global, biased toward mid-to-large cap.

Why This Exists

Everyone says they want quality. Most quality funds own the same 50 mega-caps and call it a day. MPD goes deeper — we look for the Visa of 2005, the Constellation Software of 2012, the IDEX of 2010. Companies where the compounding engine is still accelerating, not decelerating. 45 bps.
